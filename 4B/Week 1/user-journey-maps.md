# Journey Map 1  
## New Devotee → First Event → Recurring Attendee

---

## 1. Awareness

### Customer Activities
- Googles nearest temple
- Sees Instagram reel
- Clicks link to receive WhatsApp updates

### Customer Goals
- Find a nearby spiritual space

### Touchpoints
- Google Search
- Google Maps
- Social Media Channels

### Experience
Hesitant and curious

### Business Goals
- Increase awareness of temple
- Increase website & social media visits

### KPIs
- Website traffic
- Social media visits

### Responsible Teams
- Marketing

### Pain Points & Friction
- Website not optimized for newcomers
- Unclear messaging

### Drop-Off Risks
- Leaves website within 10 seconds

### Optimization Opportunities
- SEO for “Hindu temple near me”
- Clear navigation for new users

---

## 2. Consideration

### Customer Activities
- Browses website
- Looks for current events and updates

### Customer Goals
- Understand what to expect

### Touchpoints
- Website
- FAQs
- Communication channels

### Experience
Interested but slightly overwhelmed

### Business Goals
- Increase event engagement

### KPIs
- Time on site
- Event page visits

### Responsible Teams
- Website Design + Marketing

### Pain Points & Friction
- Event details unclear
- Too much information

### Drop-Off Risks
- Overwhelmed by too many details

### Optimization Opportunities
- Add “New Here?” section
- FAQ + short welcome video

---

## 3. Decision

### Customer Activities
- Decides to attend
- Registers

### Customer Goals
- Feel confident attending for the first time

### Touchpoints
- Event calendar
- Confirmation messages

### Experience
Hopeful but slightly nervous

### Business Goals
- Increase registrations

### KPIs
- Registrations
- SMS opt-ins

### Responsible Teams
- Marketing + Admin

### Pain Points & Friction
- Long registration form
- No reassurance messages

### Drop-Off Risks
- Anxiety about attending alone

### Optimization Opportunities
- Simple registration form
- Automated reminders

---

## 4. Event Experience

### Customer Activities
- Attends temple
- Interacts with volunteers

### Customer Goals
- Feel welcomed and spiritually fulfilled

### Touchpoints
- In-person greetings
- WhatsApp invitations

### Experience
Inspired and connected

### Business Goals
- Deliver high-quality experience

### KPIs
- Attendance rate
- Feedback score

### Responsible Teams
- Volunteer Coordinators + Event Leads

### Pain Points & Friction
- No personal greeting
- Seating confusion

### Drop-Off Risks
- No follow-up → forgets temple

### Optimization Opportunities
- QR check-ins
- Pair newcomers with loyal attendees

---

## 5. Loyalty & Advocacy

### Customer Activities
- Attends repeatedly
- Joins communication channels
- Encourages others

### Customer Goals
- Feel belonging
- Stay connected

### Touchpoints
- Email newsletters
- Reminder messages

### Experience
Committed and joyful

### Business Goals
- Increase repeat attendance
- Improve retention

### KPIs
- Repeat attendance %
- WhatsApp subscribers

### Responsible Teams
- Marketing + CRM

### Pain Points & Friction
- Inconsistent communication
- Feeling unnoticed

### Drop-Off Risks
- Feeling disconnected

### Optimization Opportunities
Structured follow-up sequence:
- Thank You
- Upcoming Event
- Community Invite
